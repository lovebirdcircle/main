LoveBird Circle Vision

LoveBird Circle is a nonprofit, nonreligious initiative with a vision to strengthen and foster humanity's relationship with the Healing Power of Christ in a non-dogmatic way based on an Anthroposophical understanding of the world.

Guiding Principles

Lovebird Circle wants to be an organization in service to the spiritual world where our initiatives are brought forward out of inner work and striving to understand, communicate and realize spiritual ideals.

We are in the early stages of our journey and are currently identifying our initial areas of focus.

Here are a few key principles guiding our work:

  • Non-dogmatic connection with Christ
  • Anthroposophical understanding of human development and the evolution of consciousness
  • Respect for each individual's freedom and spiritual journey
  • Integration of artistic and therapeutic practices
  • Love and enthusiasm for the spiritual world
  • Dedication to holistic soul-life and healthy development throughout all stages of life
  • Service to those in need

Developing Missions

LoveBird Circle has many different areas of interest that we are considering or starting to adopt as formal missions. Here's our current list:

  1. Celebrate Christian Festivals.
  2. Support Waldorf-based education.
  3. Develop a Waldorf-like education for adults.
  4. Establish conversational study/support groups: Sophia-Michael Goodwill Circles.
  5. Support Anthroposophical Arts and artists in general with a special focus on Eurythmy, creative speech, and art therapy.
  6. Assist those in need through existing Anthroposophically inspired programs such as Emergency Pedagogy and new Threefold Social Order forms.
  7. Foster the growth of Anthroposophy and The Christian Community.
  8. Create centers for social meetings, artistic performances, The Christian Community sacraments, and Anthroposophical studies.

Current Projects

We are actively working on several initiatives as we build our foundation and begin serving our community:

  • Building organizational infrastructure and governance
  • Developing educational and marketing materials
  • Focusing on fundraising and outreach
  • Creating a network of volunteers and supporters
  • Planning our first Christian Festival celebration: Michaelmas 2025!
